My Favorite Application

When asked about my favorite app, I had to choose one among the many apps that I use daily. I ended up choosingObey Me!One master to rule them all.I have very few chances on talking about why this app was one of my best downloads. For starters, Obey Me is an otome game where the user’s main goal is falling in love with one of the characters. The story is about the main character, which is the user, who was transported to the Devildom as an exchange student from the human world. MC will get to stay in the House of Lamentation, where the seven brothers live. The seven brothers are the avatars of sin: Lucifer, Mammon, Leviathan, Satan,Asmodeus, Beelzebub, Belphegor. The game also show other characters from time to time. The other characters are Diavolo (Devildom Prince), Barbatos (Diavolo’s Servant) Simeon and luke (Exchange Student from the Celestial Realm), Solomon (Exchange student from the human world), and Lilith (Sister of the 7 brothers).

Since Obey Me is an otome game, the target audience are the female population. Among the other otome games that I have played, Obey Me had more features that made sense to the audience. During the beginning, theuser will be given an introductory to the game. The D.D.D. is the Devildom’s version of the android system. Whenever the user logs in, they will be redirected to the home screen of their D.D.D. device. The feature I liked about the home screen is that youcould choose which character gets to greet you.

Obey Me is a story-based game. The user has toplay battles in order to receive awards and unlock stories, or lessons as they are called in the game. The game has a gacha mechanic wherein the user has to pay using grimms, devil points, and demon vouchers to get cards. The power of the cards are determined by the rarity levels: UR+ > UR > SSR > SR > R > N. Based on the photo above, there is an app called Nightmare inside the game. This is where the user can summon cards. Since this is a gacha game, there are times where it is really hard to get cards ofa higher level. The UR and UR+ cards are the hardest to collect. There are events where you need to make a certain number of summons to get 100% chance of receiving a UR card.

There is another interesting feature in the game. Based on the real world’s Instagram, Obey Me offers an app called the Devilgram. Behind every photo in the devilgram, the user can unlock stories whenever they summon the cards needed. Each part of the story can be unlocked by using keys. The devilgram shows first the cards of the current event. Figure 5 shows the devilgram focusing more on Mammon. The game held an event celebrating the birthday of the lovable character.
