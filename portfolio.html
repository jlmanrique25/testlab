Hello, I am Janna Manrique

I am a BS Computer Science student from Asia Pacific College. I was accepted last January 2020 to become a Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador As a student ambassador, it is my goal to learn more about the features that technology has to offer and share it to those who do not have the proper resources to study. I am interested in the field of Artificial Intelligence and have been following up different AI programs that can be used to help others. It is my dream to one day be recognised as part of the advancement in the field of AI.

I got to met other Filipino Microsoft Student Ambassadors through an event hosted by Microsoft. We were given a chance to help during the event. During our breaktime, we even got to talk with one of the speakers. The event was primarily for employees from public sectors. The employees from private sectors were only allowed in if they had pre-registered.
