My First Weeks of USERDES

The first week of this school year was filled with introductions. Introductions usually consisted of a room full of students introducing themselves one by one. This serves as the first time where I had to introduce myself in front of a device, while my classmates and professor listens on the other side using their own devices. USERDES was one of the very few subjects who had an interesting way of introducing students. We were asked about our favorite app, and had to show others why they liked the certain app. My chosen app is an otome game called Obey Me! One master to rule them all. The game used to be just another game that helped me pass the time. USERDES helped me further understand how design is not all about complimenting colors and texts. Design plays a bigrole in attracting users or customers. Since otome games usually cater to the female popularity, Obey Me had to focus on creating characters with different personalities.Otomegames have to take into account the possible personas of the users. Back in senior high, my friends and I chose to create a visual novel app for our app project. Our midterm app is about a guy and his pet dog. We had to take into account the people who might use our app. The personas consisted of dog lovers. Our finals app was my favorite part. As the writer of the story, I had to come up with a story that will catch the attention ofusers. I love reading romance novels. It took me a few weeks to write thestory. The stress was worth its since the story was well received by the panel of judges. I am planning on asking permission from my teammates if I could post our app on my personal website. One of my goals is to create my own visual novel apps.

Design Thinking Process

It was through this lesson that I realized that there is more to design than I thought.I initially thought that design was about creating works that are visually, appealing and this is mostly true. But for our USERDES class, we found out that User design is important since when designing something.Designers should not focus on what they think is beautiful and creative, but should focus on what their users or customers would find appealing. The design thinking process helpsdevelopers understand their users more and to help determine possible challenges that may arrive. This was a process I wish my team and I had used before. The apps and website I created before were all made based on what my team and I wanted to see. We never had a proper prototype and such. Our beta test even consisted of one of our own members who had little contribution to our project. The design thinking process can be a real eye opener to developers especially if thought at an earlier stage. We were asked to create an website that may serve as our online portfolio. The design thinking process helped me determine how to organize the contents of my website. I determined how others could easily navigate through my site and at the same time find it visually appealing.

Need Finding

This reflection is about how our group worked together to create a possible solution to certain problems. Our group consisted of 3 members. Amongthe many problemswe had thought of, we chose to go withthetransportation problems that arose this pandemic. Thepandemic has taken a toll on different industriessuch as tourismand public transportations. Since public utility vehicles couldn’t operate with full passenger capacities, both passengers and drivers are affected. Traffic in the Philippines has already been an important topic even before the pandemic happened. Passengers’waiting time has increased and it wasour goal to help lessen the struggle. We had little difficulty when we made the experience map, mostly because everyone in our group frequently used PUVs as a means oftransportationfor getting to school or other locations. This experience sort of helped us when it came to making personas for our prototype. It was also the personas that we had to create storyboards about. I couldn’t do much when it came to the design or prototype. Art was always a difficult subject for me. All I could do was think of how our users couldnavigate through our website. We even decided to create a website at first, but then we realized how it would be better to create an app since it would be easier for commuters to access compared to awebsite.
